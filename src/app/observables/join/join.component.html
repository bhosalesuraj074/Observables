merge operator

merge operator combine the multiple observable into a single observable stream it emits all values from all source.

concat operator

concat operator concatenates the multiple observable, emitting values sequentially from one observable before moving to the next.

forkJoin operator

forkJoin operator that combine the emission of the multiple observable and wait for all of them to complete before emitting an array of their last value. it's often used when you need to gather the result of multiple observable and process them once they have all completed.
